Interaction of Coloreds

"If you're Black Get Back!"

2001, digital/web, Mendi+Keith Obadike

The Interaction of Coloreds is a browser-based work that administers a “digital brown paper bag test” to visitors, toying with historical systems of racial categorization and contemporary discourse around digital surveillance.
